# BAWDYHAUZ Investor And Enterprise Summary

## What BAWDYHAUZ Is

BAWDYHAUZ is a private members ecosystem for curated matchmaking, relationship wellness, private experiences and concierge-led dating. It is not a generic swipe or hookup app. Access is application-based, verified and manually reviewed.

## Problem Solved

Traditional dating platforms often prioritise volume, speed and public self-presentation. High-value members need the opposite:

- privacy
- human-led vetting
- safety and reporting
- emotional intelligence
- curated introductions
- premium offline experiences
- support around relationship readiness

BAWDYHAUZ creates a controlled, human-led ecosystem around these needs.

## Target Audience

- Privacy-conscious professionals.
- High-intent singles.
- Members seeking quality over volume.
- People who value wellness, discretion, private spaces and curated social access.
- Venue, therapist and experience partners serving a premium adult audience.

## Differentiation

- Manual approval and verification before access.
- Matchmaking plus concierge operations.
- Relationship wellness built into the ecosystem.
- Private experiences and member gatherings.
- Human-led moderation and audit trail.
- Safety and trust positioned as product architecture, not an afterthought.
- External shop remains separate from the app experience.
