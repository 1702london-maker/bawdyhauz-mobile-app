# BAWDYHAUZ Business Model And Operations

## Business Model

Membership:

- Standard
- Black
- Elite
- Founding Member

Future monetisation:

- subscription memberships
- concierge date planning
- private experience access
- therapist/wellness bookings
- venue partnerships
- brand partnerships
- external website shop referrals

Payments remain deferred until the Stripe phase.

## Concierge Operations

Flow:

1. Members match.
2. Private chat matures.
3. Video introduction is scheduled.
4. Post-video decision can request concierge.
5. Members submit city, atmosphere, timing and privacy preferences.
6. Admin/concierge team prepares venue options.
7. Members select option.
8. Concierge confirms booking.
9. Post-date review and safety feedback are collected.

## Therapist And Wellness Operations

Flow:

1. Member views wellness section.
2. Member selects therapist/support profile.
3. Member requests session.
4. Admin/team reviews and confirms.
5. Future payment/calendar/video provider integration can be added.

No medical claims should be made in product copy.

## Venue Operations

Venue partners can support:

- private dinners
- cocktail lounges
- members spaces
- wellness venues
- discreet social mixers
- conversation salons

Future partner management can include availability, commission model and quality reviews.

## Event Operations

Private experiences support:

- invite requests
- RSVP
- waitlist
- QR placeholder
- admin guest review
- public website event sync

## Support Operations

Support should handle:

- application questions
- verification questions
- account status
- report follow-up
- event/concierge logistics
- privacy and data requests
- deletion/account closure requests

## Admin Team Model

Core roles:

- membership reviewer
- verification reviewer
- concierge coordinator
- therapist/wellness coordinator
- experience/event coordinator
- safety/moderation reviewer
- support lead

All sensitive actions should be recorded through audit logs or admin notes.
